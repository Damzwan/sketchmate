Adjust the order of objects on your canvas.

1. Enter select mode.
2. Choose the object(s) to layer.
3. Tap the vertical three dots for options.
4. Pick from:
    - "Move to front"
    - "Move 1 layer up"
    - "Move to back"
    - "Move 1 layer down"
